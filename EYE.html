Before you begin
  • 01Use a laptop or desktop monitor. Results on phones may be inaccurate.
  • 02Have a credit card or standard bank card nearby for calibration.
  • 03Sit upright with the screen at eye level.
  • 04Test each eye separately — cover one eye with your hand (not a patch) without pressing.
  • 05Ensure the room is well-lit with no glare on the screen.
⚠ Medical Disclaimer: This tool is a screening aid only and is not a substitute for a professional eye examination. For accurate diagnosis, please consult a licensed optometrist or ophthalmologist. Accuracy depends on proper screen calibration and correct viewing distance.
